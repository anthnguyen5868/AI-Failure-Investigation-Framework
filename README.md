#AI Failure Investigation Framework
Applying quality engineering principles to the investigation and evaluation of AI system failures.

#Purpose
Applying quality engineering principles to the investigation and evaluation of AI system failures.

#Questions of Interest
  Why do language models fail?
  How can failures be reproduced?
  How should mitigations be evaluated?
  How can reliability be improved?
  
#Proposed Investigation Workflow
  Observe failure.
  Reproduce failure.
  Generate hypotheses.
  Collect evidence.
  Test mitigations.
  Re-evaluate.
